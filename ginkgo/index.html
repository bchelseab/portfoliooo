ginkgobioworks.com

Ginkgo Bioworks is the company that put synthetic biology on the map, and their digital brand has evolved over the years to match their incredible growth. I've worked closely with the Ginkgo team to redesign much of their site, developing a refreshed interpretation of their brand that incorporates more motion, high-impact typography, and fluid layouts.

Website

Over the course of a year, I worked with my team to overhaul a majority of the pages on the Ginkgo site. We defined a new library of modular blocks that spoke to a bolder, more experimental brand identity.
