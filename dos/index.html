Chelsea Borg is a web designer, Boston native, and graduate of Massachusetts College of Art and Design. Though trained in graphic design, she has an equal curiosity for front-end development and makes a hobby out of writing JavaScript functions. This experimentation has resulted in creations such as an Instagram simulator game, and a choose-your-own-adventure about the end of the world, real practical stuff. This entire portfolio site, along with all of its JS functions, was lovingly handcoded by Chelsea.

Between design work and coding projects, Chelsea finds time to take ballet classes, cook extravagant meals for one, and hate the Yankees.
