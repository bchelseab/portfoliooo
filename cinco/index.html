Chelsea Borg is a student at Massachusetts College of Art and Design, anticipating the completion of her BFA in Graphic Design in 2019. Native to the city of Boston, she embraced a deep love of web design at a young age. After attending an HTML summer camp in the fifth grade, she was drawn to the intricate problem solving of coding, and spent the next decade tinkering with her HTML and CSS skills. By her third year at MassArt, Chelsea had begun the painstaking process of teaching herself Javascript, and practiced writing out her own functions with the frequent assistance of StackOverflow. This entire portfolio site, along with all of its JS functions, was lovingly handcoded by Chelsea.

When not coding, Chelsea works as a hand-painted sign artist, and hones her craft in digital design, branding, advertising, and packaging design with unbridled enthusiasm.
