Emulate Bio

Year

2021 - 2022

Role

UI design

emulatebio.com

Emulate, a company that has revolutionized biological research by creating living human in vitro models known as organs-on-chips, asked us to redesign their website to show off their incredible technology.

As part of a team, I helped to design a sleeker, more polished site, which incorporates motion where needed to engage and inform their audience.

Desktop and mobile screens

I designed multiple pages on the Emuulate site, as well as modular blocks that are used throughout the site to build out information-heavy pages. I designed a scrollthrough animation page to demonstrate their Human Emulation System, which smoothly transitions through frames on both desktop and mobile devices.
