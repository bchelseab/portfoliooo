STR

Year

2021

Role

Logo design, UI design

str.us

STR has been working in the technology and cybersecurity world for a long time, but their brand was starting to look outdated. Our team was tasked with refreshing STR's identity and website to better reflect their cutting-edge industry.

I took the lead on breathing new life into STR's logo. While the client initially hesitated to deviate too far from their familiar mark, I was able to update and improve what they liked about their logo, creating a unique badge design.

Collaborating with the design team, I helped to define and create a new style of imagery for STR, using bold geometric shapes and linear technical details to convey abstract concepts. We designed a new UI using this colorful, dynamic visual language.

The process

I started with the existing STR logo, and reworked the letterforms to have more geometric regularity.

The final product

Using the new and improved wordmark, I created a badge logo, contained in a teardrop that is part of the brand's new library of shapes.

New graphics

I assisted in creating graphics that use layered symbolic details to illustrate difficult-to-visualize technical ideas, and made photos of the team pop with brightly-colored cutouts.

Desktop and mobile screens

The library of bold colors and shapes that we used to revitalize the brand allowed us to highlight the collaborative, human-focused nature of STR as a workplace.
